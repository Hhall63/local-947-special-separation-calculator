# Product

## Register

product

## Platform

web

## Users

Active sworn Greensboro firefighters planning a future retirement. The calculator is written for firefighters themselves, without assuming help from HR or benefits staff.

## Product Purpose

The **Local 947 Special Separation Calculator** gives a firefighter a private, plain-language estimate of Fire SSA eligibility and benefit value. A successful visit takes about three minutes and leaves the firefighter knowing whether they appear eligible, why they are or are not eligible, and, when eligible, their estimated annual, biweekly, and total benefit.

## Positioning

Get a private, plain-language estimate of your Greensboro Fire SSA eligibility and benefit using your projected service, sick leave, and retirement salary.

## Brand Personality

Trustworthy, straightforward, and firefighter-proud. The interface uses the Local 947 identity with restraint so the benefit guidance remains the focus.

## Anti-references

Do not resemble a dense government or pension form, a commercial insurance or financial-sales page, or gimmicky firehouse styling. Avoid flames, sirens, distressed typography, and excessive red.

## Design Principles

- Put the calculation first and integrate Local 947 branding around the task.
- Explain eligibility decisions in plain language instead of presenting an unexplained verdict.
- Keep sensitive information in the browser and require no account or submission.
- Prefer familiar form controls and a short, guided flow that a firefighter can complete without assistance.
- Make estimates visibly distinct from official benefit determinations.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Meet WCAG 2.2 AA. Support keyboard and screen-reader use, visible focus states, large touch targets, reduced motion, and strong text contrast. Never communicate eligibility or errors by color alone, and ensure the palette remains distinguishable for common forms of color-vision deficiency.
